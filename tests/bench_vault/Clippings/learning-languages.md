# The Fastest Way to Learn a Language

> [!abstract] Summary
> Front-load the most frequent words, then drown yourself in comprehensible
> input. Speak early and badly. Treat the language like a map you uncover by
> exploring, not a syllabus to complete in order.

## The high-frequency core

A few thousand words cover the overwhelming majority of everyday speech. Learn
those first and you can understand far more than the effort suggests. Grammar
follows naturally once you have enough input.

## Comprehensible input

Consume material just above your level — understandable with effort. Shows,
podcasts, graded readers. Volume matters more than perfection.

## Speak early

Output fixes errors faster than more study. Being understood badly beats being
silent and correct.
