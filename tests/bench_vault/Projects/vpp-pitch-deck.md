# VPP Pitch Deck

> [!abstract] Summary
> The deck that tells the VPP story to investors: the problem, the wedge, the
> market, and the ask.

## Goal

Land a seed round. Ten slides, one idea per slide, a clear ask at the end.

## Tasks

- [ ] Problem slide — quantify the pain.
- [ ] Wedge slide — why us, why now.
- [ ] Market slide — bottom-up sizing, not top-down hand-waving.
- [ ] Ask slide — amount, use of funds, milestones.

## Notes

Keep it visual. Investors skim. The strongest decks read like a story, not a
spec sheet.
