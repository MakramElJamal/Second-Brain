# Fitness

## Training

Progressive overload is the whole game: add a little weight, a rep, or a set over
time. Consistency beats intensity. Compound lifts first, isolation later.

## Recovery

Sleep is the most underrated performance enhancer. Protein around 1.6–2.2 g/kg
of bodyweight supports recovery and muscle retention in a deficit.

## Supplements that earn their place

- Creatine monohydrate — the most studied supplement there is.
- Caffeine — pre-training, in moderation.
- Vitamin D and omega-3 if your diet is short on them.

Most other supplements are noise. See the creatine clipping for the brain-side
benefits beyond strength.
